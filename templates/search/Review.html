The faculty community at IIM indore is a healthy amalgam of residential and eminent visiting faculty as well as resource person from eminent colleges, industry experts and retired faculty members from various Indian and foreign universitites. These faculty members are enabled to realise their full potential through various through the many initiatives offered to them in all aspects of the institute, be it teaching, reasearch or consultancy. Under its focus on participant-centered approach to management education, IIM Indore, over the years, has deputed 21 of its faculty members to attend the Participant-Centered Learning Programme at Harvard Business School, Boston.
